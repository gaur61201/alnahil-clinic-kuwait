Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is laser hair removal safe for all skin types?

Yes. At Al-Nahil Clinic we use advanced laser systems suitable for all skin types and tones, including darker skin common across Kuwait and the Gulf.

How many sessions do I need to see results?

Most clients see significant results after 4–6 sessions. The exact number depends on your skin type, hair type, and treatment area. Dr. Alaa will advise during your initial consultation.

What is the difference between skincare and dermatology?

Dermatology is the medical diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions and diseases. Skincare focuses on cosmetic enhancement — improving texture, tone, hydration and appearance. Al-Nahil Clinic offers both under one roof.

Do you treat sensitive skin?

Absolutely. Dr. Alaa Al-Harbi specialises in personalised treatment plans carefully tailored to sensitive skin types, ensuring safety and comfort throughout.

Is there any downtime after laser treatments?

Most laser treatments have minimal to no downtime. You may experience mild redness for a few hours after your session. Full aftercare instructions are provided.

Do you offer consultations before treatment?

Yes, we always recommend beginning with a consultation with Dr. Alaa Al-Harbi. This allows us to assess your skin, understand your goals, and design the right treatment plan for you.

How much do treatments cost?

Treatment costs vary based on the procedure and number of sessions required. Please contact us directly or book a consultation for a personalised quote.

Can I book an appointment online?

Yes! You can submit a booking request using the form below, or message us directly on WhatsApp at +965 6003 0620 for the fastest response.

What should I do before my first laser session?

Avoid sun exposure for 2 weeks prior. Do not wax or pluck the area. Shave the area 24 hours before your session. Avoid applying creams or perfumes on the day. Our team will guide you through full preparation instructions.

Are your devices medically certified?

Yes. All laser and skincare devices at Al-Nahil Clinic are medically certified and approved for use in Kuwait. We invest in the latest technology to ensure the safest and most effective outcomes for our clients.
